Peanut Butter & Jelly Overnight Oats

Peanut butter and jelly is a classic combination for a reason. This oatmeal combination is an instant winner! It comes together quickly and easily, and makes for a perfect wholesome breakfast that can be enjoyed on-the-go!

Ingredients

Overnight oats

  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • ½ cup unsweetened soy milk
  • 2 Tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 Tbsp whole chia seeds
  • ¼ tsp ground cinnamon
  • ½ cup frozen mixed berries

Toppings

  • ¼ cup unsweetened coconut yogurt
  • ¼ cup fresh mixed berries
  • ½ Tbsp roasted unsalted peanuts

Directions

  1. Add the oats, milk, peanut butter, chia seeds, and cinnamon to a mason jar or airtight container. Mix to combine.
  2. Then add the frozen berries, and gently mix again. Seal and let sit overnight in the fridge.
  3. The next morning, add your desired toppings and enjoy!

Notes

Nutrition tip: aim to use plant-based milk that is fortified with calcium

Storage

Keep in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 2 days.
